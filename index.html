SERVER RULES
THE BASICS

LANGUAGE: Being an Australian based server our primary language is English. You must roleplay in English.

DDOS: Any attacks or threats of attacks will be reported to the Police and legal action may be taken.

CHEATING: Any form of cheating identified by staff will result in a permanent ban. This is not appealable.

FINAL RULING: Administrators have the final say. You can appeal punishments on Discord.

ZERO RETALIATION: Let staff handle players; you can be punished for breaking rules in retaliation. Use @ [User] [Rule Broken].

IMPERSONATION: Impersonating staff will result in a permanent ban. This is not appealable.

MIC ABUSE: Outside of a legitimate roleplaying scenario, any disruptive audio will result in a mute. Reoffenders may be banned.

EXPLOITATION: Any abuse of tools, map glitches, or newly discovered exploits are against the rules.

A SAFE ENVIRONMENT

BULLYING: Any form of mistreatment results in a permanent, unappealable ban. No second chances.

HATE SPEECH: Zero tolerance for racism, homophobia, or discrimination regarding religion, politics, and personal beliefs.

DOXING: Doxing threats will result in a permanent ban.
